Instructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on the surface and two cards are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.

The Memory Card Game is great for exercising your brain. It requires players to use the portion of their brain responsible for retreiving and storing information. By playing this game you will build upon your ability to memorize information which is important for external tasks such as learning a new skill.
